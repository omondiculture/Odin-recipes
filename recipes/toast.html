Super Simple Cinnamon Toast

Home

Description

This is the ultimate quick comfort food. It takes less than five minutes,costs almost nothing and tastes incredible with a hot cup of tea or coffee.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter(softened)
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Toast the bread:Put your bread slices into a toaster until they are golden brown and crunchy
  2. Mix the sugar spice:While the bread toasts, stir the sugar and ground cinnamon together in a small bowl
  3. Butter and sprinkle:Spread the softened butter evenly over the hot toast, then immediately shake the cinnamon sugar Mix over the top so its melts into the butter.
