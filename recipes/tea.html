How to Make A Delicate Milk Tea

Home

Descriptions

A comforting, rich and traditional cup of hot Tea.This recipe uses a strong black tea based combined with whole milk and sugar for the perfect morning drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 black tea bag (or 1 teaspoon loose tea leaves)
  • 6 ounces water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (or honey)

Steps

  1. Boil the water:Bring your water to a rolling boil in a kettle or small pot
  2. Steep the tea:Pour the boiling water ino your mug over the black tea bag. Let it steep for 3 to 5 minutes so the tea becomes strong enough to handle the milk.
  3. Add milk and sweeten:Remove the tea bag, pour in the warm milk, and stir in your sugar until it completely dissolves.
